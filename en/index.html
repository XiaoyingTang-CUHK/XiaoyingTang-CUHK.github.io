Welcome to T-lab

T-lab focuses on cutting-edge artificial intelligence research, particularly in machine learning, computer vision, and data mining. Our research is driven by multi-source big data from the internet, social media, sensors, text, images, audio, and video, dedicated to combining theoretical and practical applications.
