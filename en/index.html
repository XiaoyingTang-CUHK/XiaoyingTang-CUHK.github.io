Welcome to T-lab

T-Lab, led by Prof. Xiaoying Tang at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, focuses on research in artificial intelligence, intelligent transportation and electric vehicle scheduling, as well as privacy protection and fairness mechanism design. The lab has published high-impact work at top conferences such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, CVPR, and in leading journals including IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics. Our research covers adaptive client sampling, multi-gradient fair optimization, causal event modeling for video understanding, vehicle-grid-PV collaborative scheduling systems, and differential privacy mechanisms. T-Lab is dedicated to advancing core technologies in multimodal intelligence, distributed collaboration, and privacy protection, driving the deep integration of AI theory with applications in smart cities, energy management, and data science for real-world innovation.
