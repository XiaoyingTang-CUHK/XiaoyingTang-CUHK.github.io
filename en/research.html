Research Areas

T-lab is committed to cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence, covering multiple important directions:

Core Research Areas

Electric Vehicles

Our research focuses on intelligent control, battery management, and data-driven optimization for electric vehicles. We explore advanced algorithms for energy efficiency, predictive maintenance, and smart charging infrastructure, aiming to accelerate the adoption of sustainable transportation.

Federated Learning

We develop privacy-preserving and distributed machine learning frameworks that enable collaborative model training across multiple devices or organizations without sharing raw data. Our work addresses challenges in communication efficiency, security, and real-world deployment of federated learning systems.

Large Language Models

Our team investigates the theory, architecture, and applications of large language models (LLMs). We focus on model alignment, efficient training, multilingual understanding, and their integration into various domains such as education, healthcare, and scientific discovery.
