Lab Director

Prof. Xiaoying Tang
Assistant Professor, School of Science and Engineering
Director, T-lab

B.Eng., Honors College, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)
Ph.D., Department of Electronic Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)
Postdoctoral Fellow/Researcher, CUHK & École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL)
Prof. Tang is a faculty member at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. Her research interests include medical image analysis, brain imaging, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. She has published extensively in top journals and conferences, and serves as an associate editor for several international journals.
